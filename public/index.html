Our Story

About Women's Post 644

Women's Post 644 is one of the nation's proud all-women posts of The American Legion — an organization founded on the principles of patriotism, honor, and service to our veterans and community.

Our members are veterans and patriots who continue to serve long after their military careers have ended. From advocating for veterans' benefits to volunteering in our community, we are committed to making a difference every day.

Whether you're a veteran looking for a community of fellow service members, or someone who wants to support those who served, we welcome you to join our growing family.

Become a Member

Join Women's Post 644

The American Legion is open to veterans who served honorably in the U.S. Armed Forces. As an all-women's post, we offer a unique community of fellowship, shared service, and sisterhood.

Annual membership dues support our programs for veterans, children's scholarships, community outreach, and more.

Eligibility Requirements

  • U.S. Military veteran (any branch)
  • Served honorably during an eligible wartime period
  • U.S. citizen or U.S. national
  • Active duty service members are also eligible
